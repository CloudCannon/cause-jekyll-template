Our Mission

We are focused on providing reliable, superfast, next generation access internet connectivity for the Great Hucklow parish.

We currently provide fibre connectivity to the villages of Great Hucklow, Grindlow, Abney, and Abney Grange.

Recently we have connected the local Gliding Club, at the top of Burs Mount.
