Our Mission

We are focused on providing reliable, and superfast internet connectivity for the Great Hucklow parish.

We provide this fibre connectivity to the villages of Great Hucklow, Grindlow, Abney, and Abney Grange.

We also connect the local Gliding Club, at Burs Mount.

If you live or work in the area please contact us.

Having a fast and reliable internet connection is paramount for the Derbyshire and Lancashire Gliding Club. It allows us access weather, Notam and other websites so that we can plan our flying operations safely. A small business in today’s world is reliant on great connectivity to ensure that it is in touch with all its customers. Hucklow Net has provided a service that not only meets all our needs and expectations but significantly exceeds them and we are extremely happy to be part of this community project.

Great Hucklow Gliding Club

Having a good internet connection has improved the prospects of letting our industrial units and offices in Great Hucklow. These offices allow people to work in the local area, keeping jobs in the Peak District. This in turn keeps people buying lunches and mileage down, travelling a few miles rather than travelling into Sheffield, Buxton, or Manchester. The project has also had a wider impact on the local community, keeping young people in the area.

Pete - Local business owner

The stress and strain of not having proper Internet access to complete homework and study for GCSE’s meant my child and other children in our village were at a massive disadvantage compared with their peers. Study time and research time was almost doubled with the poor access provided by BT which led to very grumpy and frustrated children. As parents we felt very in adequate to help until HucklowNet offered to supply our village with superfast fibre. This meant a lot of hard work for adults and children alike with days and days of digging and pulling but it was so worth it. The whole village is now benefiting. 5 GCSE students are now eagerly awaiting their results this summer with five more that will be studying hard over the next few years, the whole process being so much easier with your services. Thank you so much for taking on Abney it is amazing how something so small can be so life changing.

Paula - Local resident
